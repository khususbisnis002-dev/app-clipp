Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

App Clipp collects the following information when you use our Service:

  • YouTube video URLs you submit for processing
  • Account information you provide during registration
  • Usage data such as clips generated and upload history
  • Technical data including IP address, browser type, and device info

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the App Clipp Service
  • Process and generate video clips as requested
  • Communicate important service updates
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Third-party services required to operate App Clipp (AI APIs, cloud storage)

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. Processed video clips are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of generation. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

Third-Party Services

App Clipp integrates with third-party APIs including Google Gemini, Groq, OpenAI, and TikTok. Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their data use.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure API key storage. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
